My archive, which I started to develop by collecting games, demos, programs, utility, tools, collecting and categorizing from right to left with the Commodore 64 computer that I met in 1987, by exchanging what I don't have with someone else, Atari ST in 1988, Amiga 500+ in 1991, PC in 1992. I continued with the Amiga 1200 in 1994.

In 1999, with the help of the unlimited possibilities opened by the emulation world that developed with the internet, I made an effort to ensure that the enormous archive, which was formed, is permanently located somewhere.

23 years have passed so far and where am I from where I started, even I am lost and I don't know what to do.

This website is about scene, old computers from 80's. Spectrum Sinclair, Amstrad CPC, Oric, Commodore 64, Atari ST, Amiga 500, x86 PC's, AdLib, Sound Blaster, Roland MT-32 sound cards and more...

Yes, this page is old, pure HTML, some PHP...But more than this, all the graphics on the webpage template done with Commodore 64...

If you think this website sucks, check the credits and you may see what it is about.

But now, get real, get high, scene will never die!
